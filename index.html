How It Works

It’s simple! Follow these easy steps to start your recycling journey with Ecodrop.

Download the App

Get our user-friendly app on your smartphone to get started.

Submit Recyclables

Use the app to locate drop points and submit your recyclables.

Earn Money

Get paid based on the weight of your recyclables.

Track Progress

See your recycling and earnings update in real-time.
